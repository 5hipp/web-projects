How it Works

Bug Hunter offers an experience unlike that of other website in the field, a place where users of all skill levels can practice their skills through a number of company driven bug bounty challanges and CTFs or learn new skills through the learning centre by getting hands on experience with industry grade machines. All within the comfort of your web browser with fully integrated VMs!

1

Companies Contact

Companies contact us to host development verisons of the softwares or systems within our website, as a learning resource or to be used within the CTFs or Bug Bounty Challanges.
2

Bug Searching

The uploaded softwares or systems can be either hunted by the community for bug bounty and CTF rewrads, or learned from by using and experementing with. 
3

Bug Rewards

These companies can put forward rewards in return for bugs and security related issues found within the software and systems they've uploaded.
4

Industry Results

By interacting with industry standard computer systems and software, you will build a predefined knowledge base of how companies in the industry work, meaning your applications and know how with these companies is larger than competitors, offering better job opportunities in the future.
About Agency

Bug Hutner is a free and open source project developed by Joseph Shipp for his Computer Security Dissertation during his third year at university.

Bug Hunter aims to be a professional grade website applicable to all levels of Computer Security knowledge, with areas deidcated to the development of skills within the early stages of Computer Security and dedicated bug bounty challanges and CTFs for those just wishing to sharpen their skills. So whatever your skill level, there will be something for you.
